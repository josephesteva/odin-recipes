Pasta!!!

Description

A simple spaghetti and meat sauce dish that is heavy on the meat!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Thin Spaghetti
  • 45 oz Jar of Traditional Prego Tomato Sauce
  • 2 lbs 90-95% Lean Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and place on stove on high to heat water to a boil.
  2. Add 1 tsp salt to water once heating process begins
  3. Add 1 tbsp olive oil to a second pot and set to medium heat.
  4. Add ground beef to second pot and use a turner to break up and begin browning the meat.
  5. The pot of water should come to a boil during the browning process. Add spaghetti to pot and let it boil for approximately seven minutes.
  6. While the spaghetti is boiling, continue browning ground beef until no pink meat remains.
  7. Add entire jar of sauce and stir in thoroughly. Turn heat to low and cover with lid. Stir occasionally. Start 15 minute timer.
  8. Test spaghetti after seven minutes and remove from from burner when spaghetti is cooked to desired consistency. Drain spaghetti strainer.
  9. Add two tbsp of butter in small chunks and stir into spaghetti until melted and evenly distributed. Cover spaghetti while sauce finishes.
  10. Ensure sauce remains at a simmer and stir occasionally. Remove from burner 15 minutes after sauce was added.
  11. Let cool for several minutes and serve topped with Parmesan cheese!
